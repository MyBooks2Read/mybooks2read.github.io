In Euclid- ean space, there are two kinds of projections: a central projection, where projection rays come from a point in space (figure 4.16), and a parallel projection, where they all go in parallel (figure 4.17). In projective space, these two types of projections are the same type! The center projec- tion is the same, but for the parallel one, the center lies on the infinite belt.
