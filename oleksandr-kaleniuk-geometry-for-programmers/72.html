48 The geometry of linear equations Systems of linear equations are everywhere. In fact, we solved one in the first chap- ter. Remember the two-trains problem? solution = solve([ Vp - Va * 2, Va * 1 + Vp * 1 – 450 ], (Va, Vp)) Yes, this is a small system of linear equations. It has two equations, which makes it a system, and each equation is a sum of scaled variables supplied optionally with a number, which makes equations linear.
