166 CHAPTER 6 Polynomial approximation and interpolation 6.2.1 Maclaurin and Taylor series A derivative of a polynomial is also a polynomial. That’s nice to know, but what’s even nicer to know is that the integral of a polynomial, a reverse of differential, is also a polynomial. We can exploit this feature to model whole functions solely with their derivative values. Let’s see how.
