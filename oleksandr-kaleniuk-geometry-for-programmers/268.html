We can reuse them as code in our own projects. To do so, we should put the guiding points in the expressions either in precomputation or at run time, get all the coefficients, and use the coefficients to build polynomials for both x and y. This approach works—so far. Let’s raise the degree of our transformation to see whether we can keep our approach.
